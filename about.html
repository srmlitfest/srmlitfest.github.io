ABOUT US


Welcome to L’art pour la vie, the first ever Literary Festival organized by SRM University AP-Amaravati. Literature and society has had a very strong relationship from the beginning of time. A work of art or literature has the influence of the times and society that produces it. This reflection is either direct or the author or artist acts as a prism at times to refract the times. Hence, literature could be read and understood in order to have an understanding of a particular time and society. At L’art pour la vie we target to question and ponder upon the current reality and try to address the need for the times. We welcome you all to join us to discuss, dissent and debate as that is where the fundamental of education lies in. We educate ourselves to develop rationality and question. So, let us all come together and question and search for answers
