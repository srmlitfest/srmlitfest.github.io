SPEAKER PROFILE

Afeef Mohammed

Mohammed Afeef is a lawyer and researcher at ALF. He graduated from Institute of Law, Nirma University in 2017. He is currently practicing in various courts in Bangalore, in the areas of Family Law, Domestic Violence and Criminal Law. He also conducts legal workshops on the Rights of Muslim Women in Family Laws, both Islamic Personal Law and other secular Laws. His research Interest is primarily Religion and Law. For the panel of Gender and Society, he will be talking about the conflict between the right of religious groups to autonomy and the Individual's right to equality in the Constitution in context of Sabarimala case and even personal laws.
