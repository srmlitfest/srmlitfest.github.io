SPEAKER PROFILE

Nataraj Hulliyar

Nataraj Huliyar is a Professor of Comparative Studies and Director of Gandhian Studies, Bangalore University. He is a Short Story writer, Playwright, Novelist and Critic. His important books in Kannada are: Shakespeare Manege Banda (play), Kaamana Hunnime ( Novel), Tradition and Modernity in African and Kannada Literature ( Comparative Studies), Gaali Belaku ( Cultural Essays), Maaya Kinnari ( Short Stories), Kannadi ( Column Writings). He has edited more than 40 books which include 8 volumes of Rammanohar Lohia's works in Kannada translation.
