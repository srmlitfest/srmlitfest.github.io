EVENTS

Human Library Hyderabad

2:00PM TO 5:00PM
BOTH DAYS

The Human Library is designed to build a positive framework for conversations that can challenge stereotypes and prejudices through dialogue. The Human Library is a place where real people are on loan to readers. Human library from Hyderabad will be joining us with 8 books about topics related to the event and will engage students in 20 mins session (each) where students can listen to the people and interact with them. It is a place where difficult questions are expected, appreciated and answered

Poetry's Palette

2:00PM TO 5:00PM

"There are no rules of architecture for a castle built in the clouds" Poetry's Palette is a competition capturing the very essence of art, combining the mind's eye and reality. It requires the participant to recreate the crux of the poem, given on the day, into a physical manifestation. This event would run parallelly to the other two competitions.

  • Artists will have to bring their own required materials.
  • Chart papers will be provided.
  • Time limit is 2 hours.
  • Participants will have to create their rendition of poem provided.
  • Uses of mobile phone will not be allowed during the competition hours.

Slam poetry

2:00PM TO 3:30PM

Slam poetry competition and extempore is open to all colleges invited. Mr. Tenzin Tsundue shall be opening the event by presenting his poems.

  • Each poem must be of the poet's own construction.
  • Each poet gets four minutes (plus a ten-second grace period) to read one poem. If the poet goes over time , points will be deducted from the total score.
  • The poet may not use props , costumes or musical instruments.
  • Topics will be given on the spot
  • Each speaker gets 3-4 minutes to present her/his ideas. If the speaker goes over time, points will be deducted from the total score.

Extempore

3:30PM TO 5:00PM
  • Topics will be given on the spot.
  • Each speaker gets 3-4 minutes to present her/his ideas. If the speaker goes over time, points will be deducted from the total score.
