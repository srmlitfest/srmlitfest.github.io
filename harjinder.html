Harjinder Singh

Mr. Harjinder Singh (Laltu) is a Professor at the Center for Computational Natural Science and Bioinformatics, IIIT- Hyderabad. Although his specialty is focused on Theoretical, computational chemistry, biology and the others, he is also an exceptional poet who has written more than 400 poems, a great story writer who has published more than 30 stories in reputed newspapers.
